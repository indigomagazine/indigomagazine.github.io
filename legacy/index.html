NOT

To scream at a void of grotesque inaction. To oppose what exists, what lacks, what overwhelms, what oppresses. Our world is made of both rage and hope.

Do you ignore it?
Do you revel in it?

Maybe you'd rather dance the pain away—out of sight, out of mind. Ignorance is so blissful. There is too much of everything; it feels as if there is nothing at all.

NOT is born from this chaos, from the desperate need to say no, to resist, to refuse.
